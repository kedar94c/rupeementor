How to Budget and Plan Like a Desi Pro

Ready to boss your finances? Here’s your step-by-step playbook:

  1. Track Your Rupees: Log income (job, gigs, YouTube) and expenses (rent, Zomato) for 30 days using apps like our free budget planner or Money View.
  2. Use the 50/30/20 Rule: Allocate 50% to needs (EMIs, groceries), 30% to wants (chai runs, Netflix), and 20% to savings/debt repayment.
  3. Set SMART Goals: Plan specific goals like “save ₹1,50,000 for a bike by July 2026” with ₹12,500 monthly savings. See how to prioritize.
  4. Automate Savings: Set up SIPs or auto-transfers to high-yield accounts like Jupiter or Kotak 811.
  5. Stay Insured: Get health and term insurance to avoid medical bill shocks—69% of Gen Z lack coverage, don’t join them!

Hack: Try “loud budgeting” (TikTok’s fave trend) by sharing goals on Insta’s #DesiFinance for accountability.

Top Financial Goals for 2025

Here’s what’s trending for your squad, based on 2025 insights:

  • Crush Loans: Clear high-interest debt (average ₹12,00,000 for Millennials, ₹6,00,000 for Gen Z) to save on interest.
