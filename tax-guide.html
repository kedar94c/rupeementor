Tax Guide for Indian Investors

This guide covers the old and new tax regimes, including slabs and tips for optimizing your taxes.

Old Regime Slabs

  • Up to ₹2.5 lakh: Nil
  • ₹2.5–5 lakh: 5%
  • ₹5–10 lakh: 20%
  • Above ₹10 lakh: 30%

New Regime Slabs

  • Up to ₹3 lakh: Nil
  • ₹3–6 lakh: 5%
  • ₹6–9 lakh: 10%
  • ₹9–12 lakh: 15%
  • ₹12–15 lakh: 20%
  • Above ₹15 lakh: 30%
